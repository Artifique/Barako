ANIMATIONS — Framer Motion

Page transitions: fade + subtle translateY(12px → 0), 350ms ease-out

Scroll-triggered reveals (useInView, once:true):
- Cards: stagger children 60ms, fade + translateY(20px → 0)
- Stats: count-up numbers
- Section headers: slide from left 30px

Micro-interactions:
- Job card hover: translateY(-4px) + box-shadow intensify, 150ms
- Button press: scale(0.97), 100ms
- Filter chip add: spring bounce scale(0 → 1)
- Profile avatar: subtle gradient border rotation (2deg/s, continuous)
- Tab switch: orange/green indicator slides horizontally (layoutId)
